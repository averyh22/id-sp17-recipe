Fancy Ramen for Poor College Students

Total Time:
20m
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients:

  • favorite flavor of packaged ramen
  • 2 eggs
  • small piece of beef/chicken
  • garlic powder
  • cheap vegetables of your choice (carrot, bean sprouts, corn, green onion, spinach)
  • soy sauce, seaweed, sesame seeds (optional)

Directions:

  1. In deep pot, hard boil both eggs for around 10 minutes.
  2. Wash and cut all veggies into small slivers
  3. Cook meat properly and cut into thin pieces.
  4. Prepare ramen according to package instructions.
  5. Pour finished ramen into bowl.
  6. Peel and cut eggs in half, putting them on top of the ramen.
  7. Place other ingredience on top, allowing them to sit in the hot broth to fully cook and absorb the flavor.

Enjoy!
